Overview

This document explores the Economy of Words — the art of maximising impact while minimising linguistic overhead. From the telegraph’s character limits to modern digital constraints, brevity has evolved as both necessity and art form. I examine cognitive principles, historical contexts, and practical techniques for achieving clarity through concision.

Remember: in a world of infinite content and finite attention, precision isn’t a luxury — it’s essential.
