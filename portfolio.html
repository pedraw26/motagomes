Mota Gomes

Multidisciplinary Designer — London, UK

I'm a research-led visual designer building thoughtful, contemporary work for brands and digital products. With over a decade of experience spanning startups, enterprise, and deep tech, I bring a strong conceptual foundation to every project before moving into execution.

My work sits at the intersection of design systems, brand identity, and product thinking. I care deeply about composition, typography, and detail — the invisible scaffolding that makes design feel intentional rather than decorative.

I've led design across full product lifecycles: from early-stage identity and positioning, through interface design and prototyping, to production-ready systems that scale. Every engagement starts with research and ends with something that ships.
