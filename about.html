About

I enjoy working across different parts of the creative process, from early concept and visual direction to polished execution. Recently, with the rise of AI tools, I have been deepening my workflow with Claude AI, Figma, web design, app design, and front-end experimentation. This has helped me move faster from idea to prototype, and from prototype to more complete digital experiences.

I’m especially drawn to interactive design and the way motion, layout, code, and emotion can come together to make something feel intuitive, useful, and memorable. I’m currently developing my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and p5.js, with a growing interest in building experiences that are not only visually strong, but also thoughtful and human.

Outside of work, I’m calm, caring, and naturally curious. I love spending time with family and friends, but I also value time alone to learn, experiment, and teach myself new skills. I’m someone who enjoys helping others, sharing ideas, and growing through every project I take on.

Looking ahead, I’m excited to join a creative team where I can contribute with strong visual thinking, keep learning from talented people, and help shape meaningful visual experiences at scale.
