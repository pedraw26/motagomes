One-word check-in

Relief, without overthinking

When your mind starts to spiral, the longest way out is thinking your way out. The body offers a shortcut.

Breathe in through your nose for four counts. Let the exhale stretch to eight, slow and thin, like fogging a mirror. As the breath lengthens, your nervous system reads it as safety.

Repeat for two minutes. Nothing to fix, nothing to solve. Just air doing the quiet work.

If your thoughts wander, that counts as noticing.
Just come back to the exhale.
